Analytic Services Inc.

I spent the summer after 11th grade as an intern for the Homeland Security Studies & Analysis Institute (HSSAI) at Analytic Services Inc. in Arlington, VA. HSSAI conducts research on behalf of the Department of Homeland Security. During my internship, I built and analyzed a computer simulation of the flow of illegal migrants across the U.S.-Mexico border. I taught myself NetLogo, a programming language used for creating agent-based simulations. I concluded the summer by writing a 45-page report on my findings and presented my results to my mentor's team so they could build upon my work in the future.
